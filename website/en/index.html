Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. Crohnicle has no mandatory account and sends no health data to a server. Photo analysis (Premium) goes through a service that processes the image then forgets it — it is never stored. Export and back up your data whenever you like.

Is it really free?

Yes. The whole core of the app — logs, journal, trends, scores, food associations, doctor export, backup — is free for life. Premium only unlocks unlimited photo analyses (10 free, no credit card) and, soon, voice note and weekly insight.

Is it a medical device?

No. Crohnicle is a tracking and appointment-prep tool. Scores and trends are informational self-assessments. It does not diagnose and does not replace your doctor's advice.

When will it be on the stores?

Soon. Crohnicle is being prepared for the App Store and Google Play. In the meantime the project is open source: you can follow progress and run the demo locally.

Crohnicle is not a medical device and does not replace professional medical advice. If a symptom worries you, see a healthcare professional.
